Meet the Founder

Built by someone who
just needed it to work.

A simple tool for everyday people — students, busy professionals, and anyone who's ever felt overwhelmed by complicated software.

The story

"I'm not a tech person. And for the longest time, that made managing my documents a real headache."

Like a lot of people, I spent too much time jumping between different websites just to do basic things with my files — merge a PDF here, convert a document there. Some tools were confusing to navigate, and others were just harder to use than they needed to be.

I kept thinking: why isn't there something simpler? Something that just works, without the learning curve, without making me feel like I need a degree in IT just to get things done.

"I wanted to build something I was genuinely proud of — and something I would actually use myself."

What I set out to build
Simple & clear
No confusing menus. If you can click a button, you can use AllPDFStuff.
Time-friendly
Built for people who are busy. Get in, get it done, get on with your day.
Accessible to all
No tech skills needed — if you can use the internet, you can use this.
There is a small monthly membership fee to help cover running costs and keep the site going. Thank you so much for your support — it genuinely means a lot.
My hope for you

I built AllPDFStuff with one simple goal: that you'd leave feeling like things were easy. Not just "fine" — actually easy. The kind of easy that makes you smile a little, because for once, the software got out of your way and just helped you.


If you find it useful, I'd love for you to come back. And if there's ever something you wish it could do, I'm all ears — because this tool was born from exactly that kind of wish.

Thank you for being here. I hope AllPDFStuff makes your day just a little bit easier.

D
— Darlia
