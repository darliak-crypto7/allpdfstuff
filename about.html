Our Mission

Making PDF tools
accessible to everyone

We started AllPDFStuff with one simple belief: powerful document tools should be available to everyone, not just those who can afford expensive software subscriptions.

Every day, millions of people need to compress a file to send via email, convert a document, or merge reports together — tasks that should take seconds, not require a $400/year Adobe subscription.

We built AllPDFStuff to solve exactly that. Fast, professional-grade PDF tools that work right in your browser, with a fair pricing model that starts completely free.
