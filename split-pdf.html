Why split a PDF?

Large PDFs often contain more than you need to share. Splitting lets you extract exactly the right pages — without sending the entire document.

  • Extract specific pages — pull out one page, a chapter, or any custom range from a larger file.
  • Reduce file size — sharing only the relevant pages creates a much smaller file.
  • Separate documents — split a combined PDF back into its individual component files.
  • Share confidently — send only what recipients need to see, keeping other content private.
  • Organise archives — break large scanned documents into smaller, searchable sections.
