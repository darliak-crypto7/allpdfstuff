Why convert Word to PDF?

Word documents can look different on different computers and devices. Converting to PDF locks in your formatting so everyone sees exactly what you intended.

  • Preserve layout perfectly — PDFs look the same on every device, operating system, and printer.
  • Share professional documents — PDFs are the standard format for resumes, proposals, reports, and contracts.
  • Prevent unwanted edits — PDFs can't be accidentally modified like Word files can.
  • Ensure compatibility — not everyone has Microsoft Word, but everyone can open a PDF.
  • Submit forms and applications — most online portals and job boards require PDF submissions.
