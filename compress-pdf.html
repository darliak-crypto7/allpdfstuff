Why compress your PDF files?

Large PDF files cause real problems — email providers reject attachments over 25 MB, upload forms have strict limits, and oversized files are slow to share. Compressing your PDF solves all of this in seconds.

  • Send via email — most providers cap attachments at 10–25 MB. Compress first, send instantly.
  • Upload to portals & forms — government forms, job applications, and client portals often enforce strict file size limits.
  • Save storage space — compressed PDFs take up significantly less space on your device or cloud storage.
  • Faster downloads — smaller files load and transfer much more quickly for everyone who opens them.
  • Share via messaging apps — WhatsApp, Telegram, and similar apps limit file sizes too.
