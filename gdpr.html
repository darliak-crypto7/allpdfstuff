Legal

Your GDPR Rights

Last updated: April 2026 · Effective date: 20 March 2026

In plain English: If you're in the EU, EEA, or UK, you have specific rights over your personal data. You can request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of your data at any time. We respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Union (EU), European Economic Area (EEA), or the United Kingdom (UK), you have specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR regarding your personal data held by AllPDFStuff.

Your Rights at a Glance

📋
Right of Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, including how and why we process it.

✏️
Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.

🗑️
Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data — the "right to be forgotten" — subject to legal obligations.

⏸️
Right to Restriction

Request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.

📦
Right to Data Portability

Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format to transfer to another service.

🚫
Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes at any time.

↩️
Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

🤖
Automated Decision-Making

You have the right not to be subject to solely automated decisions that significantly affect you.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To submit a data rights request, please contact us via our Contact page and select "Privacy & data" as the topic. Please include:

  • Your full name and the email address associated with your account
  • The specific right you wish to exercise
  • Any relevant details to help us locate your data

We will acknowledge your request within 72 hours and respond in full within 30 days. In complex cases, we may extend this by a further two months and will notify you accordingly.

File Data — Special Notice

Any PDF or document files you upload for processing are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 1 hour of processing. We do not retain, read, analyse, or share the contents of your uploaded files beyond what is necessary to perform the requested operation. As a result, we are unable to retrieve or provide copies of previously uploaded files.

Verification of Identity

To protect your privacy, we may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a data request. We may ask you to confirm your email address or provide other identifying information. We will not charge a fee for reasonable requests.

Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your data or your request, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority:

  • UK: Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) — ico.org.uk
  • EU: Contact your national data protection authority. A full list is available at edpb.europa.eu

Data Controller

AllPDFStuff operates allpdfstuff.com and acts as the data controller for personal data collected through the site. For all privacy and data protection queries, please use our Contact page.

For full details on how we collect, use and store your personal data, please read our Privacy Policy.
